"KIDS LEARNING ACADEMY"



This class fosters the familiarity and interest of English by immersing children in a full preschool day with native English speaking teachers and friends. Along with a monthly theme, children will sing songs, dance, go for walks, play games, read books and make crafts. We provide a total English environment identical to a typical preschool class in English-speaking countries. Children are curious about everything and often ask parents questions that leave them dumbfounded, like “Why do we have eyebrows?” or “Why do dogs sniff everything?” Knowing how things work help children make sense of the world around them. Imagine your child rushing through the door after school, all aglow because she got praised by her teacher for being able to correctly answer something that was not in any of her textbooks. This is one of the most pleasing advantages; to expose your child to a good amount of general knowledge. After all, there is nothing better than a happy and confident child!


TIME TABLE AND DESCRIPTION ABOUT CHILDREN'S PERIOD:-

TIMING SCHEDULES
10 am to 10.30am FREE PLAY TIME:
Children warm up for the day enjoying their own areas of interest. Free play is also a time when students can interact with their friends and teachers developing students' social development; such as sharing and taking turns using toys, making polite requests, and playing nicely with their peers.
10.30 am to 11am CIRCLE TIME:
Circle time includes introduction and learning new vocabulary, as well as talking about the monthly theme and about themselves. The children then join in to sing and dance with their friends and teachers.
11 am to 11.30am CALENDAR & ABC TIME:
Learn date, days of the week, season and weather by using the original calendar. Learn the alphabet through singing ABC song in a big voice and flashcards.
11.30 am to 12 noon PARK TIME:
During outdoor play, children will develop their basic athletic abilities. All children are encouraged to be active, follow traffic rules, and learn about nature and the seasons.
12 noon to 12.30 pm LUNCH TIME:
Children are asked to bring their own lunches every day. Children are encouraged to take out their lunch boxes and eat by themselves while practicing good table manners and conversing with their peers.
12.30 pm to 1 pm ACTIVITY TIME:
Children are given opportunities to develop skills like improving their hands coordination (Fine motor), actively using their bodies (Gross motor), encouraging verbal and listening skills (Language & Communication), and develop thinking skills (Cognitive & Creative skills). Children learn many new words under monthly theme such as nature, society as well as different cultures and customs.
Craft(Game): Children learn various English expressions naturally through a variety of other activities like tracing, coloring, drawing, gluing and folding, etc.
Language Arts: Children have workbooks and worksheets that introduce the lesson's theme, incorporating letter, language recognition and sentences, counting numbers, colors and shape differences, and more..
